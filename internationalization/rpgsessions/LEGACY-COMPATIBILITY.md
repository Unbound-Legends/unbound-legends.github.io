# Legacy compatibility paths

The sibling `en.json`, `fr.json`, and `es.json` files predate the active translation bridge in this directory. They remain frozen so unknown consumers do not receive a surprise 404.

Current Weblate source and target files live under `source/` and `translations/`. Do not edit or import the top-level legacy files. Exact archival copies and hashes are recorded under `../legacy/`.
